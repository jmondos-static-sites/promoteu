About Us

PromoteU comes from a business class called Foundations of Management and Entrepreneurship which requires the creation of a profitable business. PromoteU is a new kind of promotions business. Face to face meetings and personal selling combined with an optional distribution service make an exceptional experience. PromoteU provides innovative products that are especially appealing and effective in spreading a brand name.

As college students, the company knows what products appeal to students and young professionals. This is the core of our service. The company's ability to relate to prospective students or clients and provide them with what they want enables it to distribute your promotional products to your market in innovative, fun ways.
